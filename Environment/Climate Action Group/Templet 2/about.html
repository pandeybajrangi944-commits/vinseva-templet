Our Story

How EcoShield Began

Founded in 2010 by a group of environmental scientists and activists, EcoShield Foundation started with a simple goal: to protect the last remaining wild spaces on our planet. What began as a local reforestation project in the Amazon has grown into a global movement.

Today, we work with thousands of volunteers and partner with international organizations to implement sustainable solutions that benefit both people and the planet.

Our Vision

A world where humanity lives in harmony with nature, ensuring a healthy planet for all future generations.

Our Mission

To protect ecosystems, preserve biodiversity, and empower communities through science-based conservation and advocacy.

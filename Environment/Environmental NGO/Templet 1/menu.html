Conservation Initiatives

What We Do

Anti-Poaching

Deploying highly trained ranger units and advanced surveillance to protect endangered species from illegal poaching.

Habitat Restoration

Restoring critical ecosystems through reforestation and the protection of natural water sources for wildlife.

Species Monitoring

Using satellite tracking and camera traps to monitor population health and migration patterns of key species.

Wildlife Rescue

Providing emergency medical care and rehabilitation for injured or orphaned wildlife before returning them to the wild.

Community Aid

Empowering local communities with sustainable livelihoods and education to reduce human-wildlife conflict.

Policy Advocacy

Working with governments to strengthen wildlife protection laws and create more national parks and sanctuaries.

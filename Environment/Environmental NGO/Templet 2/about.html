Who We Are

Guardians of the Natural World

Founded in 2005 with a deep commitment to biodiversity, WildGuard Foundation has been at the forefront of the global conservation movement. We started as a small group of wildlife biologists and have grown into a worldwide network of conservationists.

We believe that every species plays a vital role in the health of our planet. Our journey is dedicated to ensuring that future generations inherit a world rich in wildlife and thriving ecosystems.

Our Mission

To protect endangered species and restore their habitats through science-based conservation, anti-poaching initiatives, and community-led restoration projects.

Our Vision

A world where humans live in harmony with nature, and where all species are protected and allowed to thrive in their natural environments.

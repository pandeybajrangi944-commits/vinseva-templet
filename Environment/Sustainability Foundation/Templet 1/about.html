Our Story

Restoring Earth's Vibrant Ecosystems

Founded in 2010 with a vision to heal our planet, EverGreen Foundation has grown from a local tree-planting initiative into a global force for environmental conservation.

We work at the intersection of scientific research, community development, and ecological restoration. Our goal is to create a world where humans and nature coexist in a regenerative and balanced relationship. We don't just plant trees; we foster resilient communities and protect the very foundation of life on Earth.

Science-Driven

Our restoration efforts are guided by rigorous data and ecological research for maximum impact.

Global Partnerships

We collaborate with governments, NGOs, and local leaders to scale our conservation projects worldwide.

Our Mission

To protect and restore Earth's vital ecosystems through science-led action, community empowerment, and global advocacy, ensuring a sustainable future for all living beings.

Our Vision

A world where humanity thrives in harmony with nature, where biodiversity is celebrated and protected, and where every community is resilient to environmental change.

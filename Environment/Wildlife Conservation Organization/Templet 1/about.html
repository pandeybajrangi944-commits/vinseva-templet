Our Origins

Driven By Science,
Sustained By Passion

Founded in 1995, WildGuardians began as a tactical response to the escalating poaching crisis in sub-Saharan Africa. What started as a single field unit has evolved into a global network of conservationists, scientists, and policy experts.

We don't just advocate; we act. Our teams are on the ground in over 30 countries, working 24/7 to monitor vulnerable populations, dismantle illegal trade networks, and restore habitats that have been lost to deforestation and climate change.

Our Mission

To protect the world's most vulnerable species from extinction through science-based field operations, community-led conservation, and global policy advocacy.

Our Vision

A future where wild populations of all species are stable and thriving, supported by local communities who act as the primary stewards of their natural heritage.

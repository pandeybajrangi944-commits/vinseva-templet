Our Story

A Legacy of Trust and Financial Empowerment

Founded in 2010, SOUHARDA CREDIT was established with the vision of creating a member-owned financial institution that prioritizes community growth over corporate profit. What started as a small initiative by local residents has now grown into a multi-purpose co-operative serving thousands of members.

We believe in the power of collective growth. By providing accessible credit, secure savings options, and expert financial guidance, we empower our members to achieve their dreams and build a secure future for their families.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide transparent, affordable, and accessible financial services to our members. We strive to foster a culture of savings and responsible credit management for sustainable community development.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the most trusted and preferred co-operative financial institution in the region, recognized for our commitment to member welfare and socio-economic empowerment.

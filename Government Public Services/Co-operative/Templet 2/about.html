Our Legacy

Serving the Community Since 1995

Souharda Co-operative was founded with a vision to provide accessible and ethical financial services to all sections of society. Over the past three decades, we have grown into a symbol of trust and stability for thousands of families.

We operate on the principles of mutual help and co-operation. Our focus has always been on empowering our members through financial literacy and providing them with the tools they need for economic growth.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and ethical financial solutions that foster economic self-reliance and improve the quality of life for our members and the community at large.

Our Vision

To be the leading co-operative financial institution in the region, recognized for excellence in service, financial stability, and positive social impact.

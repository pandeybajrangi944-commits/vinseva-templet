Our Story

Building Community Since 1952

GreenField Cooperative was founded by a group of neighbors who believed in the power of unity. What started as a small savings collective has grown into a thriving community organization serving over 15,000 members across the region.

For more than seven decades, we have remained true to our founding principles: democratic ownership,公平待遇, and community development. Every decision we make puts our members first.

Our Mission

To improve the quality of life for our members and community through affordable services, financial education, and collaborative programs that build lasting prosperity for all.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted cooperative in the region, known for excellence in community service, member satisfaction, and making a positive difference in every neighborhood we serve.

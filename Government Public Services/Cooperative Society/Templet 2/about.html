Our Story

Empowering Communities Through Cooperation Since 1965

GreenField Cooperative Society was founded by a group of local farmers and workers who believed in the power of collective action. What started as a small grain storage cooperative has grown into a thriving member-owned financial institution serving thousands of families.

Our mission is to promote the economic well-being of our members through affordable financial services, community development initiatives, and sustainable cooperative principles. Every decision we make puts our members first.

Our Mission

To improve the financial quality of life for our members by providing accessible savings accounts, affordable loans, and community development programs that uplift the entire community.

Our Vision

To be the leading cooperative society recognized for financial inclusion, community empowerment, and sustainable growth while adhering to the seven cooperative principles.

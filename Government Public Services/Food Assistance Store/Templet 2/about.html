Ensuring Food Security

Public Distribution System (PDS)

The Public Distribution System (PDS) is an Indian food security system established under the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution.

Our primary objective is to manage the distribution of essential food grains to the poor at highly subsidized rates. Through a network of Fair Price Shops (FPS), we ensure that no citizen goes hungry and nutritional security is maintained across the nation.

Our Mission

To provide essential food grains at affordable prices to all eligible households.

Our Vision

A hunger-free India where every citizen has access to nutritious and quality food.

Our Department

Ensuring Food Security for the Nation

The Department of Food and Public Distribution is committed to ensuring the food security of the nation through the efficient procurement, storage, and distribution of food grains. We work tirelessly to support farmers and provide affordable food to every citizen.

Our mission is to maintain a robust public distribution system that is transparent, accountable, and responsive to the needs of the people. We leverage technology to enhance the efficiency of our operations and reach the most vulnerable sections of society.

Our Mission

To ensure food security for the country through timely procurement and distribution of food grains at affordable prices, supporting both producers and consumers through a sustainable public distribution system.

Our Vision

A hunger-free India where every citizen has access to nutritious and affordable food, powered by a modern, efficient, and transparent food management system.

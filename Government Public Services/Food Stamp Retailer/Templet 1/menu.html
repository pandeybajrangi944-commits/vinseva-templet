Welfare Initiatives

Our Key Schemes & Services

PMGKAY

Pradhan Mantri Garib Kalyan Anna Yojana providing free food grains to over 80 crore beneficiaries.

One Nation One Ration Card

Nationwide portability of ration cards to ensure food security for migrant workers.

Rice Fortification

#SampoornaPoshan

Distributing fortified rice to combat malnutrition and anemia among the vulnerable.

Storage & Warehousing

Modernizing storage infrastructure with Silos to reduce post-harvest losses.

End-to-End Computerization

Digital transformation of PDS for transparency and leakage-free distribution.

Consumer Grievance

Dedicated portals and helplines for redressal of consumer complaints and feedback.

Empowering Every Citizen

Our schemes are designed to reach the last mile and ensure no one is left behind.

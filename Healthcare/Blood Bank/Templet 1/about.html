Our Story

Saving Lives Since 2010

Founded with a vision to bridge the gap between blood donors and those in need, Lifeline Blood Bank has been a pillar of hope for the community for over a decade. We started as a small facility and have grown into a state-of-the-art blood center where safety and care are our top priorities.

We believe that every drop of blood counts and every donor is a hero. Our journey has been one of continuous improvement, adopting the latest screening technologies and maintaining the highest standards of hygiene and ethical practices.

Our Mission

To ensure a safe, adequate, and accessible supply of blood and blood components for all patients in need through voluntary donations and advanced testing protocols.

Our Vision

To be a leading healthcare partner recognized for excellence in blood banking, donor care, and community service, ensuring no life is lost due to blood shortage.

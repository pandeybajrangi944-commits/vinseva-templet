Our Journey

25 Years of Precision In Medical Testing.

Founded in 2001, Labux Diagnostic Center has evolved from a local blood testing unit to a comprehensive NABL-accredited diagnostic powerhouse. We combine medical expertise with cutting-edge automation to provide reports that doctors trust.

Our commitment to accuracy is unwavering. We follow stringent internal and external quality control protocols, ensuring that every sample processed in our lab meets international standards of excellence.

Our Mission

To provide accurate, affordable, and accessible diagnostic services by leveraging advanced technology and a compassionate approach to patient care.

Our Vision

To be the most preferred diagnostic partner globally, setting benchmarks in clinical quality and patient-centric innovations.

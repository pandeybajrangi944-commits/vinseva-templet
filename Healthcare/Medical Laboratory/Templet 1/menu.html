Our Specialized Diagnostic Services

Clinical Pathology

Comprehensive analysis of blood, urine, and other body fluids to diagnose various medical conditions.

  • Complete Blood Count (CBC)
  • Urinalysis
  • Coagulation Studies

Molecular Diagnostics

Advanced genetic testing using PCR and sequencing technology for infectious diseases and oncology.

  • PCR Testing
  • DNA Sequencing
  • Genetic Counseling

Clinical Microbiology

Identification of pathogens and determination of antibiotic sensitivity for effective treatment.

  • Culture and Sensitivity
  • Fungal Identification
  • Parasitology

Clinical Biochemistry

Quantitative analysis of metabolites, enzymes, and hormones in serum and plasma.

  • Liver Function Tests
  • Kidney Profile
  • Hormone Assays

Hematology & Immuno

Specialized study of blood cells and immune system markers for complex disorders.

  • Flow Cytometry
  • Allergy Testing
  • Tumor Markers

Histopathology

Microscopic examination of tissue samples to study the manifestations of disease.

  • Biopsy Examination
  • Cytopathology
  • Immunohistochemistry

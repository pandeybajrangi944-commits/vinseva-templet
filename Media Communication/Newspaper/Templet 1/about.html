The Voice of the People Since 1924

Founded during a period of rapid global transformation, The Daily Chronicle has remained a steadfast observer of history. Our mission has always been clear: to provide our readers with accurate, unbiased, and comprehensive reporting that serves the public interest.

Over the decades, we have evolved from a local broadsheet to a global digital news agency, yet our core values of integrity, independence, and excellence remain unchanged. We believe that a well-informed citizenry is the foundation of a healthy society.

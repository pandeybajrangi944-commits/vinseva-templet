ABOUT OUR UNION

About 26+ Years
Advocating For Working People

ULA is a member-led labor union serving workers across manufacturing, logistics, public services, and construction. We help members enforce contracts, resolve workplace issues, and build long-term stability for working families.

Why Join United Labor Alliance

Membership means you never face workplace challenges alone. ULA provides representation, education, and a community built on solidarity and respect.

  • Contract Enforcement

    Support enforcing workplace rules, seniority protections, and negotiated benefits.

  • Representation

    Confidential help with grievances, discipline meetings, and workplace investigations.

  • Training & Leadership

    Steward training, safety education, and leadership development for members.

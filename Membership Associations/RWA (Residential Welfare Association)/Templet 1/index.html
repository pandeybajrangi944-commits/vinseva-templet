Our Mission

Protect Rights, Raise Standards, Build Power

UWTU is a member-led trade union representing workers across manufacturing, logistics, and essential services. We negotiate strong contracts, resolve workplace issues, and help members build long-term security for their families.

What We Do

Representation, Training & Organizing

Collective Bargaining

Negotiate fair wages, reliable schedules, and strong benefits with experienced bargaining teams.

Workplace Representation

Stewards and representatives support members with grievances, discipline, and job protections.

Training & Safety

Practical training for stewards, apprentices, and safety leaders to strengthen every workplace.

Member Benefits

Why Join United Workers

Membership means you never have to face workplace challenges alone. UWTU provides experienced representation, education, and a community built on solidarity and respect.

  • Contract Negotiation

    Bargaining teams fight for better wages, benefits, and fair workplace rules.

  • Grievance Support

    We help resolve disputes, protect rights, and ensure due process at work.

  • Skills & Leadership

    Training for stewards and members to build leadership and workplace safety.

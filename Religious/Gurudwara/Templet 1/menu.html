Spiritual & Community Services

Daily Nitnem & Prayers

Join us every morning and evening for Nitnem (daily prayers) and Rehras Sahib. Experience the peaceful recitation of Gurbani.

Weekly Kirtan Diwan

Every Sunday, we hold a special Kirtan Diwan where professional Ragis perform devotional hymns from Guru Granth Sahib Ji.

Free Langar

Free meals are served daily to all visitors without any discrimination. Everyone is welcome to sit together and partake in the Langar.

OUR JOURNEY

Our Sacred History

Established with the vision of spreading Guru's teachings, our Gurudwara has been a spiritual sanctuary for decades. We are dedicated to providing a space for prayer, reflection, and community service.

We follow the path shown by the Ten Gurus, emphasizing equality, selfless service, and the oneness of humanity. Our Gurudwara is open to all, welcoming everyone with open arms.

Our Mission

To provide a spiritual home for all, fostering a community built on the principles of Seva (service), Simran (meditation), and Sangat (holy congregation).

Our Vision

To be a beacon of hope and light in the community, promoting peace, equality, and compassion through the timeless wisdom of Gurbani.

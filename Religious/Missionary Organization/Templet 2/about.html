Faith in Action Since 1995

Founded on the principles of love and service, GraceFound has been working tirelessly for over three decades to bring hope to the marginalized. What began as a local outreach program has expanded into a global network of missionaries and volunteers.

We are committed to addressing the holistic needs of individuals—physical, emotional, and spiritual. Our projects are designed to create self-sustaining communities where every person can thrive and reach their full potential.

Our Faith

We believe in the power of faith to transform lives. Our work is driven by the mandate to serve "the least of these" with humility and grace, reflecting the love of Christ in everything we do.

Our Vision

To see a world where every community is empowered, every child is educated, and every soul finds peace and purpose through spiritual and social restoration.

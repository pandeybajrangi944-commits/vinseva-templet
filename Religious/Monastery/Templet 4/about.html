History & Heritage

A Legacy of Devotion

Founded in the early 20th century, St. Benedict Monastery has served as a sanctuary for those seeking a deeper spiritual connection. Our community of brothers follows the Rule of Saint Benedict, a timeless guide for living a life of humility, obedience, and prayer.

Through wars and peace, our walls have stood as a testament to the enduring power of faith. We continue to welcome all who come to our gates, offering a place of rest for the weary and a path of discovery for the seeker.

Our Calling

Our calling is to seek God in the common life, through common prayer and common work. We strive to be a sign of God's presence in the world, living in harmony with each other and the creation.

Our Vision

We envision a world where the values of silence, hospitality, and stewardship are cherished. Our monastery aims to be a beacon of hope and a center for spiritual renewal for the wider community.

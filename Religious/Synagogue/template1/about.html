Our History

Where Faith Meets Community

Founded in 1950, Beth Shalom Synagogue has been a cornerstone of Jewish life in the city. We are a vibrant and inclusive congregation dedicated to Torah, meaningful worship, and acts of loving-kindness (Gemilut Hasadim). Our doors are open to everyone seeking spiritual connection.

Our Mission

To inspire Jewish life by providing a welcoming spiritual home where congregants of all ages can engage in prayer, pursue lifelong learning, and make a positive impact on the world.

Our Vision

To be a vibrant, inclusive congregation where everyone feels valued, deeply connected to Jewish heritage, and empowered to pass down traditions to the next generation.

Our Story

A Club Built Around Wellness, Sports, and Belonging

Vinseva Recreation Club was created to give the community a place to stay active, try new sports, and spend quality time together. What started as a simple fitness space has grown into a full recreation experience with training, games, and social activities.

We welcome beginners and athletes alike—offering programs for every age group, supportive coaching, and facilities designed to keep you moving with confidence.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make healthy living enjoyable and accessible—through quality facilities, inclusive programs, and a welcoming community for all ages.

Our Vision

Our vision is to be the region’s most loved recreation club—where fitness, sports, and friendships grow together in a safe and inspiring environment.

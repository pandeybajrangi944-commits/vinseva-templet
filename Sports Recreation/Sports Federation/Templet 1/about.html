Our Story

Serving Sport Through Leadership and Opportunity

Founded to strengthen sport across the region, the Federation brings together athletes, clubs, coaches, and officials under one unified structure. We coordinate competitions, certify programs, and build pathways that nurture talent at every age.

From grassroots participation to elite performance, our work is guided by integrity, inclusion, and excellence. We partner with schools, communities, and stakeholders to create safe, fair, and inspiring sporting experiences.

Our Mission

Our mission is to grow participation, develop athletes, and deliver well-governed competitions. We support clubs and members with clear standards, education, and programs that raise performance across sport.

Our Vision

Our vision is a thriving sports ecosystem where every athlete has a pathway to succeed, every competition is fair and safe, and our teams compete with pride on national and international stages.

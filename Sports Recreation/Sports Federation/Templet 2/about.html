Our Story

Uniting Sport Through Governance And Opportunity

We are the governing body that connects athletes, clubs, coaches, and officials. From community participation to elite performance, we provide structure, standards, and support.

Our work includes competition management, coach and official development, safeguarding, and building pathways that help athletes progress with confidence.

Our Mission

To grow participation, raise standards, and deliver fair, safe, and well-organized sport through development programs, competitions, and community partnerships.

Our Vision

A thriving sporting ecosystem where every athlete has a pathway, every club has support, and every competition reflects integrity and excellence.
